Looking for details on Credora Methodologies? You can access them from the top left menu or below links:

- [Core Concepts](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/core-methodology-concepts): An overview of methodology mechanics.
- [Token Rating Framework](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework): The framework for combining individual methodologies in the assessment of a token.
- [Centralized Custody Methodology](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework/sub-methodologies/centralized-custody-sub-methodology): Assessment of centralized custody risk.
- [Smart Contract Custody Methodology](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework/sub-methodologies/smart-contract-custody-sub-methodology): Assessment of smart contract custody risk.
- [Derivative Token Methodology](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework/calibration-layer/derivative-tokens-methodology): Covers wrapped, liquid staking, and liquid restaking tokens.
- [Stablecoin Methodology](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework/calibration-layer/stablecoin-methodology): Covers a variety of stablecoin types, including fiat backed and alternative asset backed.
- [General Token Modifiers](https://app.gitbook.com/s/0jtQ8eeguzChYC3Ubiu5/token-rating-framework/primary-layer-general-token-modifiers): Common methodology applied for all tokens. &#x20;
